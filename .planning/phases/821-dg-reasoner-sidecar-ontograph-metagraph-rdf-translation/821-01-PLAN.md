<objective>
Scaffold the isolated `dg-reasoner` sidecar (Python 3.11 + headless JRE 17) mirroring
`data-service/`, wire it into `docker-compose.yml` as an internal-only service on the existing
network, install the n10s (neosemantics) plugin on the `neo4j` service with a smoke-verify test,
and volume-mount `./ontology` read-only. This is the foundation every later plan builds on:
Plan 02's translator, Plan 03's reasoning/SHACL routes, and Plan 04's proxy all require the
sidecar package, its pinned dependency set, and the running service to exist.

Purpose: delivers success criterion 1 (sidecar as its own container with n10s on neo4j, isolated
from data-service's hot path) and the D-05/D-07/D-08/D-12 topology decisions. The endpoints
themselves are stubs here (real logic lands in Plan 03) — this plan proves the container builds,
starts, serves `/health`, reads Neo4j over bolt, and that n10s responds.
</objective>
