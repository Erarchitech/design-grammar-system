# Decision: Vendor-Neutral Rename Stays Out of v3.0 Scope

## Decision

The runtime propagation of `dgv:speckleProjectId` → `dgv:externalProjectId` (renamed in the ontology during [[sessions/2026-06-01 Ontology v6.1 vendor-neutralization|v6.1 vendor-neutralization]]) is **not** part of v3.0 — even though v3.0 Phase 7 touches the exact same 6 schema-propagation surfaces (`cypher_template.txt`, `training/dataset_schema.json`, n8n workflow prompts, `config.template.js`, `data-service/app.py`/`speckle_validation.py`).

## Rationale

Phase 7 stays scoped strictly to what `.planning/REQUIREMENTS.md` actually specifies (VTYP/SCHM items for v3.0). The vendor-neutral rename was never folded into v3.0 requirements — bundling it in just because the touched files overlap would be scope creep, however convenient the timing.

## What This Means Concretely

- `speckleProjectId` remains the live runtime property name across all 6 surfaces until a dedicated phase/milestone picks this up.
- The full surface list and original rationale live in `DG_OBSIDIAN/sessions/2026-06-01 Ontology v6.1 vendor-neutralization.md` under "Runtime Divergence Note."
- Future sessions touching these same 6 files for unrelated reasons should not silently bundle this rename in either — it needs its own explicit phase.
