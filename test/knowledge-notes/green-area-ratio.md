# Green Area Ratio

All developments must maintain a minimum green area ratio (GAR) of 0.3. Green roofs contribute 50% of their area toward the GAR calculation. Permeable paving contributes 30% of its area. Vertical gardens on facades contribute 20% of their surface area.

Projects exceeding a GAR of 0.5 qualify for a 10% floor area bonus. Heritage trees on site must be preserved; removal requires a 3:1 replacement ratio with native species.
