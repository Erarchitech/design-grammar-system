# Facade Materials and Finishes

Primary facades facing public streets must use at least 60% natural materials (stone, brick, or timber cladding). Glass curtain walls are permitted up to 40% of the street-facing facade area. Reflective glazing with more than 20% reflectivity is prohibited on south-facing elevations.

Metal cladding panels are permitted on secondary facades only. All exterior materials must achieve a minimum fire resistance rating of Class B-s1,d0 per EN 13501-1.
