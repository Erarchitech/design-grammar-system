# Building Height Regulations

The maximum building height in Zone A is 75 meters. Buildings adjacent to historical districts must not exceed 45 meters. Rooftop structures such as mechanical penthouses and elevator overruns are excluded from height calculations if they do not exceed 5 meters above the roof line.

Setback requirements increase proportionally with building height: for every 10 meters of height above 30 meters, an additional 2 meters of setback from the property line is required.
