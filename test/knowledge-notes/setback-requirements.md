# Setback Requirements

Minimum front setback: 6 meters from the street boundary. Side setbacks: 3 meters minimum for buildings under 20 meters, 5 meters for buildings over 20 meters. Rear setback: 8 meters from the rear property line.

Corner lots require an additional 2-meter setback on the secondary street frontage. Underground structures may extend to the property line provided they are at least 1.5 meters below grade.
